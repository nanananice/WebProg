TALK: Introducing the KMITL - U. of Glasgow Double Degree Program

Posted on: November 7, 2022

TALK: Introducing the KMITL - U. of Glasgow Double Degree Program in Software Engineering and Biomedical Engineering
Fri 11 November 2022, 14.00-15.30
Room ECC-804, ECC Building
School of Engineering, KMITL, Bangkok

Founded in 1451, University of Glasgow is one of the top-rank research universities in the UK. The university has long been ranked one of the world’s top 100 universities by QS and Times. In this talk, Ms. Donna Gall (Senior International Officer) is presenting an introduction to University of Glasgow, the 2+2 programs in biomedical and software engineering, and the application process. All are welcome. (No certificates are given to participants.)

Limited seats. Register before Wed 9 Nov, 6pm, at https://bit.ly/3NK3LXG
